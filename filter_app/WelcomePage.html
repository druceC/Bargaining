Welcome to the Experiment

This study is conducted by Andrzej Baranski (a.baranski@nyu.edu)
from New York University Abu Dhabi. Our objective is to analyze decision-making in economic scenarios.

Voluntary Participation

Your participation is entirely voluntary. If you withdraw or dropout at any point in the experiment, any payments are forfeited. Please carefully review the details below.

We ask that you only accept to join this study if you will complete it fully. The study may take between 15 and 25 minutes. While you are free to leave the study for any reason, we will exclude you from future studies if you decide to do so.

What You Need to Know

  1. You will engage in decision-making tasks with other participants to earn monetary rewards.
  2. Your data will be fully anonymized to ensure confidentiality.
  3. The experiment will last approximately 15 minutes.
  4. Your compensation includes a fixed fee of $2, a survey fee of $1, plus a bonus of up to $15, based on decisions, luck, and group dynamics.
  5. Bonus payments will be distributed within one week via Prolific.
  6. There are no risks associated with this study.
  7. To participate in the experiment and qualify for any payment, you must pass a comprehension quiz.

    Failure to pass the quiz or dropping out, at any point, will result in no payment.

If you agree to participate, click 'I Agree' to proceed.
