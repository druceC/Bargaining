Study Instructions

This experiment follows a no-deception ethical policy, meaning these instructions fully describe what will happen.

You must pass a short quiz to take part in the experiment.

Overview

You will be part of a group of 3 people. Each of you will propose a distribution of 30 tokens (4 tokens = 1 USD). One proposal will be randomly selected for voting. If a proposal is rejected, the 30 tokens vanish, and all members receive 0 tokens.


Experiment Details

1. Proposal

Each participant submits a division of 30 tokens. You will have {{ proposal_timeout }} seconds to submit your proposal. The total must be exactly 30, and shares must be whole numbers (0-30). The system will randomly select one proposal for voting.

2. Voting

You will see the selected proposal and the shares assigned to each member. You can vote “Accept” or “Reject.” You will have {{ vote_timeout }} seconds to cast your vote. The proposal needs a simple majority (at least 2 votes) to pass. The proposer is automatically counted as voting in favor.

If accepted:

The result will be binding. You will then be matched into new groups to repeat the proposal and voting stages. You will participate in a total of {{ num_rounds }} rounds. In each round, you will be randomly reassigned into a group of 3 people, with your subject number for each round determined randomly as well.

If rejected:

The fund vanishes, and all members receive 0 tokens.


Earnings

2 out of the {{ num_rounds }} rounds will be randomly selected for payment. Your earnings are based on the tokens you received in that round plus a $2 fixed fee. After the experiment, you will then be paid $1 for a brief survey.

Your final payment will be displayed on the final screen.
